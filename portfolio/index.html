(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: The Journy
The Journy is a board/card game, where you select up to three classes to defeat many enemies. In this game you will travel across a map to face many challenges or enemies with a deck of abilities. As you complete these events you will gain more stronger abilties to face a final boss.

(Final) Digital Game: Kings Journy
You play as a king trying to make it to the treasure found at the end of this confusing forest. You must face the challenges of moving platforms and a strange enemy moving around. As a king with magical powers you will have the power of a dash to move forward but the price for this is to be only be able to dash forwards. Make it to the end and the treasure will be yours.
